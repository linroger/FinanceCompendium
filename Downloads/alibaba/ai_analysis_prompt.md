### 6. Valuation Analysis
- Multiple-based valuations (P/E, P/S, EV/EBITDA, P/B)

### 7. Risk Assessment
- Credit risk metrics (Z-score, leverage ratios)
- Operational risk indicators
- Financial flexibility metrics

### 8. Business Segment Analysis
- Segment margin analysis and profitability
