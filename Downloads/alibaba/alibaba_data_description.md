# Alibaba Group Financial Data Description

This document describes the financial data available in the Excel file `full-tables.xlsx` for Alibaba Group Holding Limited.

## Data Overview

The data spans fiscal years 2020-2025 and includes comprehensive financial statements, segment information, and detailed financial metrics.

## Available Data Tables

### 1. Income Statement (IS)
- **Time Period**: 2020-2025
- **Currency**: RMB (in millions)
- **Key Metrics**:
  - Revenue and cost of revenue
  - Gross profit
  - Operating expenses (product development, sales & marketing, general & administrative)
  - Amortization and impairment
  - Impairment of goodwill
  - Income from operations
  - Interest and investment income/expenses
  - Income before taxes
  - Net income and net income attributable to shareholders
  - Earnings per share (basic and diluted)
  - Number of shares outstanding

### 2. Balance Sheet (BS)
- **Time Period**: 2021-2025
- **Currency**: RMB (in millions)
- **Assets**:
  - Current assets: cash, short-term investments, restricted cash, equity securities, receivables
  - Non-current assets: equity securities, receivables, equity method investees, property & equipment, intangible assets, goodwill
- **Liabilities**:
  - Current liabilities: bank borrowings, unsecured senior notes, tax payable, accounts payable, merchant deposits, deferred revenue
  - Non-current liabilities: deferred revenue, deferred tax liabilities, bank borrowings, unsecured senior notes, convertible senior notes, other liabilities
- **Equity**:
  - Ordinary shares, additional paid-in capital, treasury shares, statutory reserves
  - Retained earnings, accumulated other comprehensive income
  - Total shareholders' equity and noncontrolling interests

### 3. Cash Flow Statement (CFS)
- **Time Period**: 2020-2025
- **Currency**: RMB (in millions) with US$ conversions
- **Operating Activities**:
  - Net income, adjustments (depreciation, amortization, share-based compensation, impairments)
  - Changes in working capital components
  - Net cash from operating activities
- **Investing Activities**:
  - Short-term investments, treasury investments
  - Acquisitions and disposals of investments and assets
  - Property and equipment purchases
  - Business combinations
- **Financing Activities**:
  - Share issuance and repurchases
  - Dividend distributions
  - Debt proceeds and payments
  - Net cash from financing activities

### 4. Business Segment Data (Table 3, Table 9, Table 15)
- **Time Period**: 2023-2025
- **Segments**:
  - Taobao and Tmall Group (China commerce retail and wholesale)
  - Alibaba International Digital Commerce Group (International retail and wholesale)
  - Cloud Intelligence Group
  - Cainiao Smart Logistics Network Limited
  - Local Services Group
  - Hujing Digital Media and Entertainment Group
  - All others
- **Metrics**:
  - Revenue by segment
  - Adjusted EBITA by segment
  - Year-over-year growth rates

### 5. Financial Ratios and Adjusted Metrics (Table 11)
- Net income reconciliation to adjusted EBITA and adjusted EBITDA
- Non-GAAP net income adjustments
- Free cash flow calculation
- Share-based compensation adjustments

### 6. Share Information (Table 13)
- Basic vs diluted earnings per share calculations
- Non-GAAP diluted earnings per share
- Weighted average shares outstanding (basic and diluted)

### 7. Tax Information (Table 23)
- Share of profit/loss from equity method investees
- Ant Group investment details

### 8. Consolidation Details (Table 24, Table 28)
- Financial information by entity type (Parent, Other Subsidiaries, Major VIEs, Primary Beneficiaries)
- Revenue and expense breakdown by entity
- Intercompany eliminations

### 9. Cash Flow by Entity (Table 30)
- Cash flow breakdown by entity type
- Parent, Subsidiaries, VIEs, and Primary Beneficiaries

## Key Financial Trends

### Revenue Growth
- 2020: 509,711 RMB million
- 2021: 717,289 RMB million
- 2022: 853,062 RMB million
- 2023: 868,687 RMB million
- 2024: 941,168 RMB million
- 2025: 996,347 RMB million

### Profitability Trends
- Improving operating margins in recent years (14.1% in 2025 vs 8.2% in 2022)
- Net income volatility with strong recovery in 2025
- Segment-level performance varies significantly

### Liquidity and Capital Structure
- Strong cash position with fluctuations
- Manageable debt levels relative to equity base
- Significant investments in growth areas
